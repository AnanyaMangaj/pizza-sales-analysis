# Pizza Sales Analysis – Business Requirements

## 1. Business Problem

The goal of this project is to analyze pizza sales data to understand the business performance of the pizza store. By analyzing sales data, we can identify trends, customer preferences, and the best and worst selling pizzas. This analysis helps the business make better decisions to improve sales and customer satisfaction.

---

## 2. Dataset Description

The dataset contains information about pizza orders, including:

* Order ID
* Pizza Name
* Pizza Category
* Pizza Size
* Quantity
* Total Price
* Order Date
* Order Time

This information allows us to analyze customer behavior and sales trends.

---

## 3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following KPIs will be calculated to evaluate business performance.

### Total Revenue

Total revenue generated from all pizza sales.

### Average Order Value

The average amount spent per order.

Formula:
Average Order Value = Total Revenue / Total Orders

### Total Pizzas Sold

The total quantity of pizzas sold.

### Total Orders

The total number of orders placed by customers.

### Average Pizzas per Order

The average number of pizzas ordered per order.

Formula:
Average Pizzas per Order = Total Pizzas Sold / Total Orders

## 5. Visualization Requirements

The following visualizations will be created to analyze pizza sales.

### Daily Trend for Total Orders

A bar chart showing how orders change across different days.

### Monthly Trend for Total Orders

A line chart showing monthly order trends.

### Percentage of Sales by Pizza Category

A pie chart showing the contribution of each pizza category.

### Percentage of Sales by Pizza Size

A pie chart showing customer preference for pizza sizes.

### Total Pizzas Sold by Category

A chart comparing sales performance of pizza categories.

### Top 5 Best Selling Pizzas

Bar chart showing pizzas with highest revenue and sales.

### Bottom 5 Worst Selling Pizzas

Bar chart showing pizzas with lowest revenue and sales.

---

## 6. Dashboard

A dynamic dashboard will be created using the Dash framework in Python. The dashboard will display key metrics and interactive charts to help stakeholders easily understand business performance.

## 7. Expected Outcome

This analysis will help the business:
